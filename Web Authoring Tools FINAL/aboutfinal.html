About Me

Hello! My name is Rachel Edler and I am a junior studying computer information systems major at the illustrious Howard University in Washington, DC. I am a Georgia peach -- raised in Stone Mountain. I am a collegiate athlete triple jumper and a natural hair connoisseur.

During my pasttime, I like to read, write down my thoughts, or create short hair tutorials (check out my IG page! @sunshine.locd). I love setting goals for myself, traveling, and learning new things about the world around me. Adventures excite me.

Upon my graduation in 2021, I would like to work for a company that I'm in love with as a UX designer. I have big plans for myself in the future, but it is most important to me that I leave a long lasting impact on my community.
